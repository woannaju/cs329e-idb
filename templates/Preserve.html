National Preserve

A National Preserve is an area of special scenic, historical, or scientific importance set aside and maintained by a national government and in the U.S. by an act of Congress.

There are 20 National Preserves in the US with the most popular being the Mojave National Preserve in California.
National Preserve Name Location Description
Timucuan National Preserve Florida Timucuan is located in northeastern Florida along the Atlantic coast. We are a Ecological and Historic National Preserve featuring 46,000 acres of wetlands, waterways, and other natural habitats.
